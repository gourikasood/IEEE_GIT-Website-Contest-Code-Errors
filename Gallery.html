PAANCHAJANYA

IEEE-GIT conducted its annual flagship event PAANCHAJANYA. This is a regional level inter-college technical symposium for students. Idea presentations, Projects, were major events. Student and Members were benefitted. The IDEA presented and few BOT based projects have been invited at the institute INCUBATION Centre for support.

Student Leadership Program

This is an annual membership drive event. Target audience: 1st and 3rd Semester students are attracted for membership, by supporting best performing student with finance for membership. Branch Counselor Prof. Vineeta P. Gejji presented about IEEE memberships its awareness, usage, advantages, opportunities to new students. Student Branch supported 1 new member with nearly 50% IEEE membership fees.



TRIGARTI – Women’s Day Celebration

It was a part of IEEE-GIT WIE affinity group. Girl students get an opportunity to showcase their creativity and business skills. Chief Guest talked about challenges for women in today’s workplace. Ladies staffs of the institute were felicitated. It helped in membership of girl students.

IEEE DAY CELEBRATIONS

IEEE-Day was celebrated. The Executive Committee members were given badges of their posts – THREE new members were added to the committee. Pinning ceremony was done thru the hands of Counselor Prof. V. G. Gejji, Dept Chair Dr. Santosh Saraf, Senior Member Dr. Deepak B. Kulkrni. Members celebrated the day with HIV affected kids in an orphanage, essential goodies were donated to them(water bottles)

GREEN DAY by WIE Affinity Group

The Executive Committee along with members of WIE Affinity Group planted SAPLINGS as a social responsibility. They took pledge to nurture it and help environment to grow GREEN.



HAM RADIO – an effective scientific hobby

One day workshop on Amateur Radio, creating your own Radio at home. HAM Radio orientation was taken. In duty HAM operators of GOA and BELAGAVI took the session to motivate students to take up HAM Radio hobby. Students learned how to prepare a small Radio Circuit and real and current satellite images were shown. Student branch members were benefitted by this workshop.

RASPBERRY-Pi WORKSHOP

IEEE-GIT for the first time conducted a week long workshop. It was a Hub Level event with students participating from various colleges in the region. Students learned to create projects and few submitted their UG projects using Raspberry-Pi. Few are still working on new projects. Members were benefitted
